Urban Rest North Sydney

Urban Rest North Sydney is located in the heart of the North Sydney business district with modern, spacious, and beautifully designed apartments, including two exclusive penthouses. All apartments come with a large balcony, brand-new appliances, and floor-to-ceiling windows. In addition, all guests have access to the shared rooftop with stunning views over Sydney Harbour.

Choose between studio, one, two, and three-bedroom apartments, all designed to the highest standards that emphasise light and space.

North Sydney is a suburb and central commercial district on the Lower North Shore of Sydney, just across the harbour from the CBD. During the weekdays, office workers fill the contemporary eateries clustered around Miller Street. On weekends, locals visit the twice-a-month Northside Produce Market for organic produce and gourmet food.

Riverside Heaven

Close your eyes. Take a deep breath in. Exhale. That’s just a taste of the zen you’ll find on this unique nature stay. Immerse yourself in nature, wind down and enjoy a more self-sufficient way of life. Enjoy fishing, swimming, kayaking, paddle boarding, biking and walking trails. This rare find is nearly two acres of land five minutes from the M4 motorway. 10 minutes from Penrith CBS and Westfields at the foot of the beautiful Blue Mountains. No facilities here. You must be fully self-contained.

Camping is a unique way to accommodate semi-self-sufficient campers. It is an exclusive retreat located on Emu Heights on the banks of the magnificent River, overlooking natural environmental waterways. It is accessible to RVs, camper trailers, caravans, and big rigs. Camping in tents is allowed. Visitors must be fully self-contained as there are no facilities available.
